Semi-Private Training

Our semi-private training classes are designed to help individuals feel more comfortable in the gym and achieve specific fitness goals.

Semi-private training is personal training in a small group setting. We highlight key aspects from personal training such as personalized training, 1:1 meetings with your coach, and individual coaching and guidance, while including the encouragement and camaraderie gained from a group training setting. Classes are small, up to five individuals, allowing for personalized coaching and guidance.

Semi-private training caters to people seeking a more personalized and goal oriented training approach. Whether you are looking for a program to help you achieve your fitness goals, haven't been to the gym in a while, or have a pre-existing injury, our semi-private training sessions have you covered.

Workouts are individualized to you and your goals. In our semi-private training, you will recieve the opportunity to have a monthly 1:1 meeting with your coach to keep you moving forward.
