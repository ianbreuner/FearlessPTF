Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest quality of care to support individuals to return to what they love to do without fear. Whether you are dealing with an injury or pain, or want to create a more balanced and healthier lifestyle, we have the expertise and tools to help you reach your potential. Through a collaborative approach, we can assist you in creating sustainable change and long-term solutions.

The Professionals

Anna Swanson PT, DPT, CSCS

Physical Therapist + Trainer

Anna Swanson, PT, DPT, CSCS, graduated from Montana State University with a Bachelor's in Exercise Science and received her Doctorate of Physical Therapy from St. Catherine University.

As a clinician, Anna takes an active approach to physical therapy, emphasizing strength, mobility, and movement. She firmly believes in movement as medicine and continuously promotes balanced and active lifestyles for both her rehab and fitness clients. Anna approaches her clients' care with specificity and compassion, helping them return to the activities they love.

Outside of Fearless, Anna is an avid outdoor enthusiast. She loves skiing, hiking, playing golf and pickleball. Her passion for an active lifestyle inspired her to pursue a degree in physical therapy and to open Fearless Physical Therapy and Fitness. Anna’s goal is to help her clients get back to the activities they love without the fear of physical limitations and pain. She looks forward to teaming up with you to achieve your goals.

Ian Breuner, CSCS

Trainer

Ian Breuner graduated with a Bachelors in Science in Exercise Science from Montana State University in Bozeman, Montana. He is a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (National Strength and Conditioning Association) and has worked as a Strength and Conditioning coach for clients of all ages and abilities, ranging from high school student athletes to older adult populations.

Outside of work, Ian enjoys a variety of recreational and outdoor sports. He is an avid skiier, hiker and volleyball player. Ian's goal is to provide a fun and educational fitness environment, helping you reach your goals and have fun along the way.
