Fearless Physical Therapy and Fitness

Fearless aims to bridge the gap between physical therapy and fitness by intrucing physical therapy clients to structured fitness classes. The team here believes in getting clients back to doing the activities they enjoy.

The Professionals

Anna Swanson DPT, CSCS - Physical Therapist + Fitness Trainer

Anna Swanson graduated with a Doctorate in Physical Therapy from St. Katherines University in St. Paul, Minnesota. She is passionate about helping clients reach their goals of returning to the activities that they love. Anna will be providing physical therapy services as well as teaching classes. When she is not working, she enjoys playing pickleball, running with dogs, lifting weights and Avatar: The Last Airbender.

Ian Breuner, CSCS - Fitness Trainer

Ian Breuner graduated with a Bachelors in Science in Exercise Science from Montana State University in Bozeman, Montana. He has worked as a Strength and Conditioning coach for clients of all ages and abilities, ranging from high school student athletes to older adult populations. Ian will be teaching classes part-time. When he is not working, he enjoys playing pickleball, running with dogs, lifting weights and Avatar: The Last Airbender.
